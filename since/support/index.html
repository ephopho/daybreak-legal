How does Since work?

Add the things you lose track of. The dashboard shows how long it's been since you last did each one. When you do it, tap Done — the clock resets to zero.

What's the $0.99 unlock?

Since is free for up to 5 things. A one-time $0.99 unlock removes the limit and turns on reminders. It's a one-time purchase — no subscription. Tap Restore purchase in Settings if you reinstall or get a new phone.

Can I get reminders?

Yes — set an interval (weekly, monthly, yearly…) on an item and turn on "Remind me when it's due." Reminders are local; make sure notifications are enabled for Since in your phone's Settings.

How do I delete my data?

Everything is stored on your device — delete an item by editing it, or delete the app to remove all of its data. See our Privacy Policy.
